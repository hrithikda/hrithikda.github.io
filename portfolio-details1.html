Project Info

  • Domain: Healthcare / Machine Learning
  • Stack: Python, XGBoost, Neural Networks, SHAP, Tableau
  • GitHub: View Repository

The Problem

Hospitals face CMS penalties when patients are readmitted within 30 days — a costly and largely preventable outcome. Administrators needed a data-driven way to identify high-risk patients before discharge.

What I Built

  • Trained regression, XGBoost, and neural network models to predict excess readmission rates with measurable accuracy
  • Built interactive dashboards with SHAP-based feature importance to explain model decisions to non-technical stakeholders
  • Delivered visualizations on discharge volumes, predicted vs. expected readmission trends, and key clinical factors

Impact

Enabled healthcare administrators to pinpoint quality gaps, redirect clinical resources, and reduce potential penalty exposure through actionable, interpretable predictions.
