Project Info

  • Domain: Quantitative Finance / ML
  • Stack: Python, Random Forest, RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, Streamlit
  • GitHub: View Repository

The Problem

Retail and quant traders struggle to identify statistically valid alpha signals from the noise of market data. Manual analysis is slow, inconsistent, and rarely reproducible.

What I Built

  • Designed an end-to-end ML pipeline integrating technical indicators (RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands) with a Random Forest classifier to generate buy/sell signals
  • Improved strategy optimization by 35% through backtesting and hyperparameter tuning
  • Built an interactive Streamlit dashboard with real-time stock analytics, model evaluation metrics, and backtesting visualizations

Impact

Delivered a reusable research platform enabling traders to evaluate, visualize, and refine quantitative strategies — cutting analysis time and improving signal quality.
