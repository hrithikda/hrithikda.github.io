Project Info

  • Domain: Digital Strategy / NLP
  • Stack: WordPress, Python, Zero-Shot Classification, NLP
  • Client: rootedindignity.com

The Problem

Rooted In Dignity, a nonprofit, had a website with poor UX and a social media presence that wasn't converting — lacking data-driven insight into what messaging resonated with their audience.

What I Built

  • Led a team of five to conduct a comprehensive WordPress website audit — improving structure, accessibility, and alignment with nonprofit industry standards
  • Applied zero-shot NLP classification to analyse Rooted In Dignity's social media posts and competitor profiles, surfacing high-performing content themes
  • Delivered a structured strategy report with actionable recommendations to increase engagement and brand visibility

Impact

Provided the organisation with a clear content roadmap grounded in competitive benchmarking and NLP-driven insight — enabling data-informed decisions without a dedicated analytics team.
